POS taggers for Estonian text

About the project

The task is to create a program that annotates part-of-speech tags in Estonian text. The input consists of a training corpus where morphological analysis and tagging have been performed. The task is divided into three main parts:
1. Corpus processing: Loading and preprocessing the corpus in a suitable format.
2. Training and testing taggers.
3. Making conclusions and analyzing the results.

Training and Testing Taggers

Five part-pf-speech taggers are trained on the corpus:
Unigram Tagger (NLTK UnigramTagger) (backoff: DefaultTagger)
Bigram Tagger (NLTK BigramTagger) (backoff: UnigramTagger - DefaultTagger)
Trigram Tagger (NLTK TrigramTagger) (backoff: BigramTagger - UnigramTagger - DefaultTagger)
Hidden Markov Model Tagger (NLTK HiddenMarkovModelTagger)
Brill Tagger (NLTK BrillTagger) (initial tagger: UnigramTagger, at least 3 templates improving UnigramTagger's results)
