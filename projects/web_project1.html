Emergency Alert App

About the project

The Wildlife Emergency Application is web application that seeks to provide a platform to collectively and cohesively coordinate the wildlife rescue efforts in Estonia. The main goal of the app will be to enable the public to easily and quickly notify the the Estonian Wildlife Center when they happen to notice an animal that may be in need of assistance or a check-up. By filling out a form, describing the situtation, a report will be sent to a central ticketing system, overseen by the volunteers at EWC (Estonian Wildlife Center), who can then respond appropriately and organize help to the animal in need.

My Role

As part of the Emergency Alert App development team, I played a multifaceted role. My responsibilities included frontend development, testing, and DevOps tasks. I contributed to the user interface using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript (React). I ensured the application's reliability through comprehensive testing, and I implemented DevOps practices for continuous integration and deployment.
